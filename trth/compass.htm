The Bible: The Ultimate Moral Compass

"Wherewithal shall a young man cleanse his way? by taking heed thereto according to thy word." - Psalms 119:9

The Bible is our compass. If we forget it or proudly forsake it believing we don't need it, we will lose our way from treasures to destruction. We may start off in the correct path, but when storms come and lead us astray, we will not know where to turn, for we have forgotten God, our compass. Therefore we will not reach treasure, but rather the winds (Satan) will hurl us against the rocks and kill us.

Therefore I warn all my brothers: hold fast unto the Word of God, so that even when storms come, you will get through them, and you will not go astray but rather go in the right path. Without God we are lost. We are sheep without a shepherd.
