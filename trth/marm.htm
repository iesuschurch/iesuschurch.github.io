Mormons Against Race-Mixing

By Iesus Church | April XXII, MMXXIII

If thou art a Mormon, thou should be against miscegenation; that is, race-mixing, for this doth the Book of Mormon say thereof: 2 Nephi 5:23

AND cursed shall be the seed of him that mixeth with their seed; for they shall be cursed even with the same cursing [a skin of blackness]. And the Lord spake it, and it was done.

Think what thou wilt, but that is not what matters; it is what God says that matters. If the Book of Mormon is truth, the quote above is truth. If God wrote the Book of Mormon, He included the aforementioned quote for a reason.

Wherefore I exhort all you, my Mormon friends, to reprove the evil of race-mixing (Ephesians 5:11) and tell others. Share this article with thy Mormon friends and priests, and if ye have any questions, Email me. Thank thee for reading and God bless thee.
