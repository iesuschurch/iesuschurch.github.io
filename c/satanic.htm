PORN IS SATANIC

"Turn away mine eyes from beholding vanity; and quicken thou me in thy way." - Psalms 119:37

Porn is in direct rebellion against God. Open rebellion against God and the praise of this rebellion is the basically satanism. It is evil, and should be made illegal.

Porn Is a Sin

Jesus Christ himself said so, for He said in Matthew 5:28

But I say unto you, That whosoever looketh on a woman to lust after her hath committed adultery with her already in his heart.

And even before that God said in Psalms 101:3

I will set no wicked thing before mine eyes: I hate the work of them that turn aside; it shall not cleave to me.

God is clear about this in His word: pornography is a sin.

Open Rebellion

Satanism is open rebellion against God. That is pornography! Shameless, unapologetic rebellion against the very God who bought (saved) them: 2 Peter 2:1

But there were false prophets also among the people, even as there shall be false teachers among you, who privily shall bring in damnable heresies, even denying the Lord that bought them, and bring upon themselves swift destruction.
