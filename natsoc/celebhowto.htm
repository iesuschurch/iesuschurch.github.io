Memorize Horst Wessel-Lied

Horst Wessel-Lied (German for Horst Wessel's Song) is a song written by Horst Wessel, a German martyr who was shot by a communist when the Nationalist Socialist Party was starting to rise to power. Joseph Goebbels then published the song and lyrics in his newspaper, Der Angriff. The song later became the co-national anthem of Germany, the national anthem being Deutschlandlied (whose lyrics or notes you can also learn for April 20th). The song is emblematic of Nazi Germany and very nice. So, instead of singing Hitler happy birthday (which I suppose you could), you should sing him Horst Wessel-Lied.
