Abortion Is Murder

"And he said, What hast thou done? the voice of thy brother's blood crieth unto me from the ground." - God Genesis 4:10

The Bible is clear on the issue: abortion is murder. We see it all the way going back to the first chapter of Genesis when God tells mankind to "Be fruitful, and multiply" (Genesis 1:28) We then see it again in Exodus when God gives the sixth of the ten commandments and says "Thou shalt not kill." (Exodus 20:13). No one should ever justify abortion. If doing so they justify cold-blooded murder of the worst kind.

This straying from God and perverting of God's word has led to the death of people since Roe v. Wade.
